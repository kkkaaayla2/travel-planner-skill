## 中文

### 🌟 核心优势

本 Skill 致力于解决传统旅行规划中耗时耗力、信息分散的痛点，为对行程精度和个性化有较高要求的旅行者提供结构化、本地化的解决方案。

- **高度结构化的本地输出**：相较于常规 AI 助手生成的冗长富文本，本工具直接输出包含 5 个 Sheet（行程、住宿推荐、机票参考、物品清单、景点介绍）的 Excel 表格。本地文件不仅便于查阅，更能有效应对境外旅行时网络信号不佳的情况。
- **精确到时间段的行程安排**：不仅提供景点建议，更会根据实际情况规划具体的游览时间段，避免行程过载。
- **详尽的实效性导航路线**：提供具体的打卡路线规划。例如：
  > **基督城大教堂广场（Cathedral Square）**
  > 打卡路线：广场中心 -> 基督城大教堂（正在修复中，外观拍照）-> 过渡纸板教堂（Cardboard Cathedral）内部参观
- **深度个性化定制**：在生成计划前，系统会主动询问并整合用户的具体偏好（如目标城市、偏好景点类型等），避免生成千篇一律的模板化行程。
- **一站式信息整合**：免去在地图软件、社交媒体和预订网站之间反复跳转确认的繁琐过程。

### 🚀 快速开始

#### 使用方法

在与 Manus 交互时，只需提及以下内容即可触发此 Skill：
- "我想去[目的地]旅行"
- "帮我规划一下去[目的地]的行程"
- "制作一个[目的地][天数]天的旅行计划表"
- 或直接上传旅行笔记、Excel、PDF 或网页链接，Manus 会自动解析并生成计划。

## English

### 🌟 Core Advantages

This Skill is designed to solve the time-consuming and fragmented nature of traditional travel planning, providing a structured and localized solution for travelers who demand high precision and personalization in their itineraries.

- **Highly Structured Local Output**: Compared to the lengthy rich text generated by conventional AI assistants, this tool directly outputs an Excel spreadsheet containing 5 sheets (Itinerary, Accommodation Recommendations, Flight References, Packing List, Attraction Guides). Local files are not only easy to review but also reliable when internet connectivity is poor abroad.
- **Precise Time Scheduling**: It provides not just attraction suggestions, but specific time slots for visits based on practical conditions, preventing itinerary overload.
- **Detailed and Time-Sensitive Navigation Routes**: Offers specific photo and walking routes. For example:
  > **Christchurch Cathedral Square**
  > Route: Square Center -> Christchurch Cathedral (under restoration, exterior photo) -> Cardboard Cathedral (interior visit)
- **Deep Personalization**: Before generating the plan, the system actively inquires about and integrates the user's specific preferences (such as target cities and preferred types of attractions), avoiding cookie-cutter templates.
- **All-in-One Information Integration**: Eliminates the tedious process of constantly switching between map applications, social media, and booking websites to confirm details.

### 🚀 Quick Start

#### Usage

Simply mention the following when interacting with Manus to trigger this Skill:
- "I want to travel to [Destination]"
- "Help me plan a trip to [Destination]"
- "Create a [Duration]-day travel plan for [Destination]"
- Or directly upload travel notes, Excel, PDF, or web links, and Manus will automatically parse and generate the plan.
